What is the best type of TV mount for my space?

No matter your TV size, from compact screens up to 60″ to massive displays over 80″, our professional mounting services guarantee a secure and stylish installation. We handle all brands and sizes, ensuring the perfect setup for any room. Enjoy a clutter-free space and the best viewing experience with our expert TV mounting solutions. Whether it’s for a cozy bedroom, a spacious living room, or a dedicated home theater, we’ve got you covered.

  • Fixed mount

    A fixed mount is a basic, stationary bracket that holds the TV flat against the wall. This is a simple and low-profile option, ideal for rooms where the viewing angle doesn’t need adjustment.

  • Tilting mount

    A tilting mount allows you to angle the TV vertically, typically downwards. This is useful for reducing glare from lights or windows and for mounting the TV higher on the wall while maintaining a comfortable viewing angle.

  • Full-motion mount

    A full-motion or articulating mount offers the most flexibility, allowing you to tilt, swivel, and extend the TV away from the wall. This option provides maximum adjustability, making it suitable for rooms with unconventional layouts or where you want to be able to adjust the TV position frequently.
